Our Story

A garden café in the
heart of Bahrain

Coco's is Adliya's beloved all-day café — a canopied courtyard where regulars linger over brunch and families gather for weekend lunches. Our kitchen blends Mediterranean flavours with international comfort food, using fresh ingredients and homemade sauces.


Every dish is made with care — from slow-cooked pasta sauces to freshly grilled mains. Great food deserves a great atmosphere, and Coco's delivers both.
